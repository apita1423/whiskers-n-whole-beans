What are toe beans? Toe beans is a nickname that was given to cats' paws because they look like jelly beans


The Cats

We strive to create a calm atmosphere for the cats in our care.

We have two residential cats that call Whiskers N Whole Beans their permanent home.

We create a calm and cosy environment for around 10-14 foster cats from the DSPCA.

We partnered with the DSPCA to foster cats that will have a bigger environment to roam and interact with customers, which can help with future adoption.

Visit the Cats page to see a gallery of some of our cats.


The Humans


Humans? It's what the cats call us.

Amanda, the owner, a librarian, is a prime example of a cat lady.

We, the humans, cater to every whim of our pet residents.

For the humans, we serve coffee and pastries to make the experience that much enjoyable.
