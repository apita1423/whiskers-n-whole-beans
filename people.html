Behind every idea there is a story...


Whiskers N Whole Beans's story started when Amanda, the "human" owner, adopted two fostered cats, Mo and Eva. Together they went through late nights finishing two degrees, and many live changes. Amanda became a librarian and was completely happy with the sterotype of librarians seem to always have cats! When Mo and Eva were adopted, they were extremely distant and took awhile for them to settle into their new home. Amanda thought, if foster cats can be in an environment that allows for interaction between people and cats, the process and transition of adopted cats would be much smoother. With that, and the love of coffee and pastries, Whiskers N Whole Beans was created. Mo and Eva are still messing around with the addition of a brother, Thorin, a labrador that keeps them on their toe beans.

Pictured: Mo (Left) and Eva (Right).


The Rescue

Whiskers and Whole Beans is partnered with the DSPCA to house foster cats; this allows for the cats to have a larger area to roam and have interactions with customers. Having interaction with customers will allow them to become accustomed to people petting or playing with them. This increases the chances for the cats to find and stay in their forever home. The process for adoption is run through the DSPCA. For more information on adopting any of the foster cats in Whiskers N Whole Beans, please visit the DSCPA's website.
