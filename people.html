Once upon a time


Behind every idea there is a story...

Whiskers N Whole Beans's story started when Amanda, the "human" owner, adopted two fostered cats, Mo and Eva. Together they went through many late nights trying to finish two degrees, and many live changes. Amanda became a librarian/archivist and was completely happy with the sterotypes of all librarians seem to have cats! When Mo and Eva were adopted, they were extremely distance and took awhile for them to become used to their new home and humans. Amanda thought, if fosters cat can be in an environment that allows for interaction between people and cats, the process and transition of adopted cats can be much smoother. With that, and the love of coffee and pastries, Whiskers N Whole Beans was created. Mo and Eva are still messing around with the addition of a brother Thorin, a labrador that keeps them on their toe beans.
